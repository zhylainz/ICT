<b>The National Flag of India (Hindi: Tiranga)</b> is a horizontal rectangular tricolour of India saffron, white and India green with the Ashoka Chakra, a 24-spoke wheel, in navy blue at its centre.
<br>
<br>
<b>The National Flag of Paraguay</b> is a red-white-blue triband, was inspired by the colours of French Tricolour, believed to signify independence and liberty. <br>The flag is unusual because it differs on its obverse (front) and reverze (back) sides: <br>the obverse of the flag shows the national coat of arms, and the reverse shows the seal of the treasury.
